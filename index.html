Rhino 3D

Rhino powers the jewelry industry’s design needs, from the smallest boutique to the world’s largest manufacturers
Rhino also serves as a host platform for several industry-leading jewelry design plugins such as CrossGems, Stuller’s MatrixGold®, and CounterSketch® to make designing even faster and more efficient

Matrix Gold

Matrix Gold is a jewelry-specific CAD software developed by Gemvision, designed for creating 3D jewelry designs. It's known for its powerful tools, including parametric history for easy modifications and a streamlined interface that combines features from Matrix and RhinoGold
MatrixGold aims to make the jewelry design process faster and more efficient for both students and seasoned designers.
